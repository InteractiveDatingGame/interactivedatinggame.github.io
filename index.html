About

Meet your CTO! Meet your Startup! Meet your VC!

Take three ambitious startups, let them pitch to one successful VC, hidden behind a wall ... and watch the magic happen! The Interactive Dating Game has it all: wit, laughter, entertainment - and a matchmaking like you´ve never experienced before.

Join us, ask yourself "Who is your Herzblatt?" and enjoy the show.

An event like German beer:

  • easy to relax
  • easy to make new contacts
  • and the best method to get honest insights

Hand-picked startups meet high-class investors in the most candid way: Questions never asked before reveal insights never given before. Who’s the perfect match for you?
Join our event series that has already made dreams come true for so many founders and investors: Matchmaking with inspiring people in a creative and fun environment. A direct analog synthesis (DAS) of efficiency, humor and entertainment.

Background

Standard startup pitches are boring - PERIOD! but there's hope - the interactive dating Game! 30 years after Steve jobs courted bill Gates we present you the 2014 reboot - premiered & battle-tested during last year's tech festival “interactive cologne”. We'll bring you back to this time. We are not searching for farmers or daughters-in-law - we present you the best and only CTO's, founders and VC's you need to meet!
